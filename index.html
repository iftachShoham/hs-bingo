📖 How to Play
High Society — Snakes & Rats · Complete Guide
Jump to:
1
The Right Panel — Your Control Centre
📍 Right sidebar

The panel on the right is where all gameplay actions happen. It is divided into collapsible sections:

  • 📍 Current Task — your active tile number and the task you need to complete
  • 🎲 Roll Dice — advance (or retreat) on the board after finishing a task
  • ✅ Complete Task — upload proof of a completed task
  • 🏆 All Teams — see every team's position, recent completions, and history
  • 📊 Statistics — charts and a leaderboard tracking completions
2
📍 Current Task
📍 Right sidebar → top section

This section always shows your team's current tile number and the full description of the task you need to complete.

The status line beneath the description shows your progress — for example "1 / 3 completions" means you need to submit valid proof 3 times before the tile is fully cleared.

Tip: Read the task description carefully before uploading proof — it tells you exactly what counts as a valid submission.
3
🎲 Roll Dice
📍 Right sidebar → Roll Dice

Roll the dice to move forward on the board. There are two situations where you need to roll:

  • When first starting — roll to get your team onto the board
  • After completing a task — once a tile is fully done, roll to advance to the next one

Click the purple Roll Dice button and the result appears below it, showing how many spaces you moved and which tile you landed on.

Tip: You can only roll when it is your turn to do so. If the button is greyed out, you likely still have an active task to complete first.
4
✅ Complete Task
📍 Right sidebar → Complete Task

Once you have finished a task, submit proof using one of these methods:

  • Click the 📁 photo/file button to open a file browser and select an image from your computer
  • Paste a direct image URL into the text field (a link that points directly to an image file)
  • Drag and drop an image file onto the dashed proof area
  • Paste from clipboard — copy a screenshot and paste it directly into the proof area

A preview will appear once an image is attached. Then click the green Complete button to submit.

Important: Your submission will be reviewed. Make sure the screenshot clearly shows the completed task.
5
⚡ Early Completion
📍 Right sidebar → Complete Task (when eligible)

Some tiles require multiple qualifying drops, while some can be completed early by obtaining a specific big drop or multiple smaller drops, ie:

A tile requiring 10 Dagannoth Rings or a pet can be completed immediately if you obtain the pet. Simply tick the Early Completion checkbox before submitting.

Important: Pets are single-use. If a pet is used for Early Completion, it cannot also be claimed as a Pet Reroll.
6
⏪ Rollback — Move Backwards
📍 Right sidebar → Roll Dice (left side checkbox)

If your team is stuck on a difficult tile, use a Rollback to move backwards and land on a different tile instead.

  • Tick the ⏪ Rollback checkbox on the left of the Roll Dice section
  • Click Roll — you will move backwards rather than forwards
  • A confirmation dialog appears before the move is committed
  • The gold badge shows exactly how many charges your team currently has
Warning: Rollbacks are limited — use them when genuinely stuck, not just for a tricky tile.
6b
🐾 Earning Rollback Charges
📍 Right sidebar → Complete Task section

There are two ways to earn rollback charges:

1. Pass Tile 40 — your team automatically receives 1 free rollback charge when you advance past tile 40.

2. Trade a Pet — if you obtained a pet while working on the current tile, tick "Trade pet for Rollback" in the Complete Task section and click Complete Task as normal. The tile will not be marked complete — your rollback count increases by 1 instead.

Pet rules: Only pets received while actively pursuing the current tile count toward this trade. A pet can only serve one purpose — tile completion or rollback credit, not both.
Note: Choosing the pet trade is a deliberate decision to give up a completion in exchange for rollback flexibility.
7
🏆 All Teams
📍 Right sidebar → All Teams

This section shows the current standing of every team in the game.

  • Each row shows the team's colour bullet (🟣🔴🔵🟡), name, and their current tile number
  • A 🔥 flame next to a name means that team is on a hot completion streak
  • Rollback charges are displayed as a small badge if a team has any remaining

At the top of this section is the ⚡ Recent Completions bar — small tile thumbnails showing the latest completions from all teams, so you can track rival progress in real time.

Click any team row to open their full completion history, showing every tile they have finished and the proof they submitted.

Tip: Watch the Recent Completions bar to keep an eye on other teams without leaving the page.
8
🗺️ The Board — Snakes & Rats
📍 Main board area

The 10×10 board has special tiles that can change your position. The colour legend at the bottom of the board labels every tile type — refer to it any time you are unsure what a colour means. Here is what each colour indicates:

Snake head — Landing here sends you sliding down the snake to a lower tile!
Revealed rat — A hidden trap tile that has been uncovered. When triggered, a wheel spins to determine which team is penalised.
Your current tile — Highlighted in blue so you can always spot your position instantly.
Completed by you — Tiles your team has already cleared, shown in green.
About Rats: Rat traps are hidden — tiles only show as rat tiles once someone has landed on one and triggered it. When triggered, a spinning wheel determines which team faces the consequence.
Hide snakes: If the snake figures make the board hard to read, tick the Hide snakes checkbox in the colour legend. The red snake-head tiles stay visible — only the drawn snake figures disappear.
9
🔍 Reading a Tile & Clicking for Details
📍 Main board area → any tile

Each tile on the board shows useful at-a-glance information:

  • Small team emoji bullets at the bottom of a tile show which teams are currently standing on it
  • A green background on a tile means at least one team has completed it
  • A progress bar shows how many completions a multi-step tile has received so far

Click (or tap on mobile) any tile to open a detail popup showing:

  • The full task description
  • Which teams have completed this tile and their proof images
  • Which teams are currently standing on it
Tip: Clicking tiles is a great way to scout upcoming tasks before you land on them, so you can plan ahead.
10
📊 Statistics
📍 Right sidebar → Statistics

The Statistics section gives you a live picture of how the competition is unfolding across all teams.

Completions per Day (bar chart) — shows how many tiles each team completed on each day of the event, colour-coded by team. Use this to spot who had a strong session and who went quiet.

Cumulative Completions (line chart) — tracks each team's running total of tile completions over time. This is the clearest way to see who is ahead in the race and whether anyone is catching up.

🏅 Top Players leaderboard — ranks individual players by the number of tiles they have personally completed, not team totals. Useful for spotting the most active contributors within a team.

The colour legend below the charts maps each colour to a team — the same colours used on the board and in the teams list. Click any chart to open a zoomed-in view with the full legend for easier reading.

Tip: The Cumulative chart is the best at-a-glance indicator of who is winning overall — the highest line at any point in time is the leading team.
